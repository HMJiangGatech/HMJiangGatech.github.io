About Me⌗

I am a 3rd year PhD student in the School of Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISyE) at Georgia Tech. Before I joined Georgia Tech, I received my B.S. Degree in Computer Science and Mathematics from the School of the Gifted Young at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC).

My research focuses on deep Learning, adversarial machine learning, natrual language processing and open source software development for scientific computing. I am working with Prof. Tuo Zhao in the FLASH (Foundations of LeArning Systems for alcHemy) research group.
