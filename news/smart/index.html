State-of-the-art Results on GLUE Benchmark

2019-12-05
#nlp  #smart  #bert  #fine-tuning  #GLUE 

Our recent collabrative work with Microsoft Dynamics 365 AI and Microsoft Research AI (paper, code) achieves state-of-the-art results in 5 of 9 GLUE benchmark tasks and an overall GLUE task performance 89.9, which outperforms all existing models.

The reuslts are summerized as follows:

Method CoLA SST MRPC STS-B QQP MNLI-m/mm QNLI RTE WNLI AX Score #params
Previous SOTA 70.8 97.1 91.9/89.2 92.5/92.1 74.6/90.4 92.0/91.7 96.7 92.5 93.2 53.1 89.7 11,000M
MT-DNN-SMART 69.5 97.5 93.7/91.6 92.9/92.5 73.9/90.2 91.0/90.8 99.2 89.7 94.5 50.2 89.9 356M
  • Previous SOTA: T5

Reference⌗

  • SMART: Robust and Efficient Fine-Tuning for Pre-trained Natural Language Models through Principled Regularized Optimization 2020
    Haoming Jiang, Pengcheng He, Weizhu Chen, Xiaodong Liu, Jianfeng Gao and Tuo Zhao [arXiv] [Code]
    Annual Conference of the Association for Computational Linguistics (ACL), 2020
